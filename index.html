HAPPY INDEPENDENCE DAY

We celebrate our Independence Day on 15th August every year.
It is the day when we became independent from the foreign rule
after a long and continuous struggle for freedom.
Minister of India unfurls the National Flag over the Red Fort in Delhi on this day.
He also addresses the nation from the ramparts of the Red Fort on this day.

After the flag hoisting, National Anthem is sung. A guard of honor
is presented by the Armed Forces, the Police and the N.C.C.,
Similar functions are held in all the State capitals and important towns and cities.
A special tribute to all the brave soldiers who gave their life for our freedom


Let us all be proud Indians to celebrate this auspicious day
